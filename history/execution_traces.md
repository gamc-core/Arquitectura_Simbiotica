# Execution Traces

Execution traces record publicly observable structural acts produced within the Arquitectura Simbiótica system.

They represent moments where structural conditions, artifacts or runtime actions become externally visible and serve as minimal historical markers documenting the evolution of the system.

---

## ET01 — Execution Trace

Initial public trace documenting structural execution conditions.

---

## ET02 — Artifact Freeze

Public release and freeze of structural artifact series.

---

## ET03 — Finite Execution Regime

Declaration of a finite operational regime for controlled system execution.

---

## CL01 — Structural Cell Concept

Introduction of the structural cell as the minimal irreversible act capable of producing system history.

---

Additional traces may be recorded as the system evolves.

Some execution traces have also been publicly observable through external publications.
